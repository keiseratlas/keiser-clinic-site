About The Keiser Clinic

A family-owned practice in Chelsea, Michigan, dedicated to finding answers when others can't.

Our Story

The Keiser Clinic was founded with a simple mission: provide the kind of thorough, personalized neurological care that's increasingly rare in modern healthcare.

We've built a practice around patients who have been dismissed, misdiagnosed, or told there's nothing that can be done. We know that's often not true — it just means the right tests haven't been run.

Today, we see patients from across the United States and internationally, specializing in complex conditions like POTS, post-concussion syndrome, vestibular disorders, and dysautonomia.

What is Chiropractic Neurology?

A chiropractic neurologist has additional post-graduate training in clinical neuroscience after their Doctor of Chiropractic degree. This training focuses on understanding how the nervous system works and how to optimize its function through non-pharmaceutical and non-surgical means.

We combine advanced diagnostic technologies — transcranial Doppler, videonystagmography, computerized posturography, eye tracking — with evidence-based neurological rehabilitation to find and treat the root cause of symptoms.

This approach is particularly effective for conditions that don't respond to traditional medical management, because we're looking at the nervous system from a different angle.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Nathan Keiser

Founder & Lead Clinician

Dr. Nathan Keiser is a board-certified chiropractic neurologist and founder of The Keiser Clinic. With advanced training in vestibular rehabilitation, dysautonomia management, and brain injury recovery, Dr. Keiser has dedicated his career to helping patients who have been failed by the conventional medical system.

He specializes in complex cases — patients who have seen multiple specialists without answers, those with persistent symptoms after concussion, and individuals with autonomic dysfunction that hasn't responded to standard treatment.
